PawMart is a community-driven platform where pet owners, breeders, and shops can list pets for adoption or sell pet-related products.

## Features
- **Adoption & Sales**: Browse pets for adoption or buy pet food and accessories.
- **User Dashboard**: Manage your listings and view your orders.
- **Secure Authentication**: Login securely with Email/Password or Google.
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop.
- **Dynamic Search & Filters**: Easily find what you need by category or name.

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: React, Tailwind CSS, DaisyUI, Framer Motion
- **Backend**: Express.js, MongoDB, 
- **Authentication**: Firebase
